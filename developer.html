Cyberpunk 2077 was launched in September 2020 on various platforms like Xbox Series X and Series S, PlayStation 4, PlayStation5, Xbox One, Google Stadia and Microsoft Windows. It was designed by Konrad Tomaszkiewicz and composed by Marcin Przbytowicz, Paul Leonard-Morgan and P.T. Adamczyk. CD Projekt Red and CD Projekt (established in 2002)developed and published it.

Cyberspunk 2077 is an adaption which drew its influences from the 1982 film Blade Runner, manga and anime series Ghost in the Shell, and video games System Shock (1994) and Deus Ex (2000). There’s a lot of visual connections between Cyberpunk 2077’s Night City and the nameless city of the Long Tomorrow novel by Leigh Brackett. Neither forgoes colour and both glow with a sense of urban grit. The Long Tomorrow ends with “just another story, There are a hundred million stories in this city; this was just 12 of them” a line that equally applies to Cyberpunk 2077 and its hyperactive switching between stories and details. 

The game also points to an undercurrent of fantasy in cyberpunk that subverts the idea that a cyberpunk city should be a horrible place-our worst possible future.
